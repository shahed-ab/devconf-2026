Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you register.

▶ What's included in my conference ticket?

Every ticket includes access to all keynote and breakout talks, workshop sessions based on your plan, meals during the conference days, and a digital copy of the slides after the event.

▶ Can I get a refund if I can't attend?

Cancellations made before August 15, 2026 receive a full refund. After that date, tickets can be transferred to a colleague at no extra charge, but refunds are no longer available.

▶ Will sessions be recorded?

All talks are recorded. Standard ticket holders get 30 days of access to recordings, while Pro and Team ticket holders get lifetime access.

▶ Is there a virtual attendance option?

DevConf 2026 is an in-person only event this year, to keep the focus on hands-on workshops and networking. Recordings will be available afterward for those who can't attend live.

▶ Do you offer group or student discounts?

Groups of 10 or more should use the Team plan. Students with a valid .edu email get 50% off the Standard plan.
